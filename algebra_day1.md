# Week 1 Day 1: Algebra Foundations

## Problem 1
**Solve**: `2x + 5 = 11`

**Solution**:
1. Subtract 5: `2x = 6`
2. Divide by 2: `x = 3`

$$2(3) + 5 = 11$$
## Problem 2 (Real Khan Academy)
**Exercise**: Solve the system:
x + y = 5
2x - y = 1

**Your Solution**:
1. Add equations: `3x = 6` → `x = 2`
2. Substitute: `2 + y = 5` → `y = 3`

**Verification**: `2(2) - 3 = 1 ✓`

$$x = 2, y = 3$$
