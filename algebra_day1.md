## Problem 1
**Solve**: $2x + 5 = 11$

**Solution**:
1. Subtract 5: $2x = 6$
2. Divide by 2: $x = 3$

**Verification**: $$2(3) + 5 = 11$$

## Problem 2 (Real Khan Academy)
**Exercise**: Solve the system:
$$
\begin{cases}
x + y = 5
2x - y = 1
\end{cases}$$

**Solution**:
1. Add equations: $3x = 6$ → $x = 2$
2. Substitute: $2 + y = 5$ → $y = 3$

**Verification**: $$2(2) - 3 = 1 \checkmark$$

$$x = 2, y = 3$$
